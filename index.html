About GK Writes

Welcome to GK Writes, your gateway to exploring the rich tapestry of history and mythology from around the world. From ancient Hindu deities to Greek gods, from Egyptian pharaohs to Norse legends, we delve deep into the stories that shaped civilizations and continue to inspire us today.

Our Focus

We specialize in bringing you comprehensive coverage of:

  • Hindu Mythology & Philosophy: Explore the divine stories of Vishnu, Shiva, Ganesha, Krishna, and the profound wisdom of ancient Indian texts
  • Indian History: From the Mahabharata and Ramayana to the great empires and cultural traditions
  • World Mythologies: Greek, Norse, Egyptian, Roman, and mythologies from across the globe
  • Ancient Civilizations: The rise and fall of great empires and their lasting legacies
  • Divine Stories: The timeless tales of gods, heroes, and spiritual teachings
  • Archaeological Discoveries: Latest findings that reshape our understanding of the past

Our Mission

We believe that the stories of gods, heroes, and ancient civilizations are not just historical accounts, but living wisdom that continues to guide and inspire us. Through well-researched articles and engaging storytelling, we make these profound teachings accessible to modern readers.

About the Author

GK Writes is dedicated to preserving and sharing the incredible heritage of human mythology and history. Our focus on both Eastern and Western traditions reflects our belief that wisdom knows no boundaries.
